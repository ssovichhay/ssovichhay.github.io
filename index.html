About

Thanks for visiting! I'm a person of simplicity—hence the simple website. Just kidding… I'm just too lazy to make a better one. But the simplicity part is true. What about honesty? It feels like a forgotten value these days. Everyone wants honesty, but no one is completely honest. I want to be remembered as someone who is honest. I lead, but I don't control.
